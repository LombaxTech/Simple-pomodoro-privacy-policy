# Privacy Policy

**Work & Break Timer**

Last updated: 19 December 2025

## Overview

Work & Break Timer respects your privacy. This app does not collect, store, or share any personal data.

## Data Collection

Work & Break Timer does **not** collect any personal information, usage data, or analytics.

- No accounts
- No sign-in
- No cloud services
- No analytics or tracking SDKs
- No advertising

All app data (such as timer settings and session counts) is stored **locally on your device** and never leaves it.

## In-App Purchases

If you choose to make an in-app purchase, the transaction is handled entirely by Apple through the App Store.  
Work & Break Timer does not receive or store any payment information.

## Third-Party Services

Work & Break Timer does not use any third-party services that collect data.

## Notifications

The app may send local notifications (for timer completion) if you grant permission.  
These notifications are generated locally on your device and are not sent to any server.
